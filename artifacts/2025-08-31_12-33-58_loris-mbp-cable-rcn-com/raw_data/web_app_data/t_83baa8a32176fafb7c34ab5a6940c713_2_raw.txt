In the film, Coach Carter’s clipboard becomes a symbol of his authority and his method.  He is constantly looking at it, checking grades, and holding his players accountable. It represents the strict, data-driven approach he takes to coaching—not just the game, but their lives. The clipboard is a constant, quiet reminder that his focus is on the numbers that will get them to college, not just the numbers on the scoreboard. LeadershipStyle DataDriven Accountability
