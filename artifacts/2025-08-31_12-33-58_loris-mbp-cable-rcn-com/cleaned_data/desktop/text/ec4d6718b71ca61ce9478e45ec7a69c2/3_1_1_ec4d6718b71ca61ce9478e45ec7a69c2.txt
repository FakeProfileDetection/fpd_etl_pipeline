A true leader’s legacy isn’t measured by the trophies they collect, but by the lives they change. In Coach Carter, Samuel L. Jackson’s character Ken Carter showed us this firsthand.  By locking the gym and demanding academic excellence, he put his career on the line to save his players' futures. The backlash from the community and parents shows just how difficult it is to challenge the status quo, but his unwavering belief ultimately proved that he saw a bigger picture for his players than anyone else did.
