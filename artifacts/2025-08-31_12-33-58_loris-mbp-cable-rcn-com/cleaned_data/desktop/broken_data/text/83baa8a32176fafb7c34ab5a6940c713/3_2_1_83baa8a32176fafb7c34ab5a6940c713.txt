The joke itself was a catalyst, but it sparked a much larger, and more complex, conversation.  Chris Rock's joke about Jada Pinkett Smith's shaved head, a result of her alopecia, was immediately criticized by many as insensitive and cruel. But for others, it was just a comedian doing his job. This moment forced us to confront our own feelings about the boundaries of comedy, where the line between humor and a personal attack is drawn, and whether a joke can ever truly justify a violent response.
